Guide to Chemical Element Categories Demo of Bootstrap panels and definition lists

Alkali metals
often are soft in texture
Alkaline earth metals
have very high melting points
Metalloids
are "poor metals"
Nonmetals
include the elements H, C, N, and O
Halogens
are very chemically reactive
Noble gases
are very stable chemically
Others are transition metals, post-transition metals, lanthanides, and actinides.
